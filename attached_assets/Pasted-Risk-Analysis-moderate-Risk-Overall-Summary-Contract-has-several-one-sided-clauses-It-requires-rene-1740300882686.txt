High-Risk Liability Clause
high Risk
Party B acknowledges and accepts full responsibility for any loss, damage, or legal action arising from misuse, non-compliance, or negligence associated with the delivered services/products. Party A shall not be held liable beyond the value of the contract, regardless of circumstances.

Risk Analysis: This clause is heavily one-sided and places all liability on Party B. It should be renegotiated to share liability.
